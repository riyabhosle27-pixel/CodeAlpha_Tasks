Netflix Data Visualization

Tools & Technologies Used
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Seaborn
Google Colab

Dataset Used
Netflix Movies and TV Shows Dataset (CSV format)

Visualizations & Graphs Used
1. Movies vs TV Shows
Graph Type: Count Plot (Bar Graph)
Used to compare the number of Movies and TV Shows available on Netflix.

2. Content Growth Over Years
Graph Type: Line Plot
Shows how the number of Netflix titles has increased over time.

3. Top 10 Content Producing Countries
Graph Type: Horizontal Bar Chart
Highlights the countries contributing the most content to Netflix.

4. Genre Analysis
Graph Type: Bar Chart
Displays the most popular genres available on Netflix after processing multi-category data.

5. Rating Distribution
Graph Type: Count Plot
Shows how content is distributed across different ratings (TV-MA, PG, etc.).

6. Movie Duration Distribution
Graph Type: Histogram with KDE
Represents the distribution of movie lengths and identifies common duration ranges.

7. Movies vs TV Shows Over Years
Graph Type: Grouped Count Plot
Compares the growth of Movies and TV Shows over different years.

Key Insights
Netflix has more Movies than TV Shows
Content production increased significantly after 2015
United States is the leading content producer
Drama and International genres are most popular
Most content falls under the TV-MA rating
Movie durations are mostly between 80–120 minutes
